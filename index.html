About

I'm a Software Engineering graduate and Research Assistant at New Uzbekistan University, working on reinforcement learning for real-world energy systems. I like turning research ideas into working, reproducible code.

Research

Energy-efficient train scheduling

Applying reinforcement learning to optimize train speed profiles and cut energy use, with simulation pipelines built from real rail trajectory, gradient, and curvature data — in collaboration with Uzbekistan Railway and funded by the Uzbekistan–China Joint Research Grant.
